Why Choose Pidipidi Kindergarten

Quality Curriculum

Our curriculum is tailored age-appropriately for each group. A blend of play-based learning, individualised activites and group activities make just the right environment for young minds to flourish.

Expert Teachers

Our friendly staff are qualified and well-trained to lay academic foundations for children, including those with special needs whose curriculum is designed by our in-house specialists.

Structured Schedule

Our reception mirros primary school. It more structure and prepares children for a smooth transition into the next level.

Safety Policies

We have clearly-outlined, and strictly implemented safety policies. These include policies on safety on the school premises, policies in case of emergencies, as well as policies that protect against bullying.

Our Educational Programs

Our Nursery Program and "Big School" class lay the foundations of the children's academic journey while aiming for holistic development.

Learning outcomes include:

  • Basic Reading and Writing Skills
  • Basic Math/Counting
  • Socially-sensitive Children
