About Us

Nurturing Young Minds since 2002

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe childhood should be a joyful adventure. We've created a nurturing space, blending purposeful play with intentional care to meet your child's developmental needs.

We promote holistic child growth through:

  • Play-based learning and hands-on activities to nurture cognitive growth.
  • Individualised Activities, tailored to meet the unique needs of each child.
  • Social-emotional development through group activities.
  • Our Reception class Program, which offers more structure to Bridge Children to Primary School Naturally

Safety Policies

Facility Security

Our facilities have gated access, and are monitored 24/7. We have strict sign-in/sign-out procedures. Only registered parents and guardians may pick up children.

Classroom & Playground Safety

We have Childproofed learning environments with age-appropriate equipment and Constant adult supervision.

Health & Hygiene

In addition to Regular cleaning and sanitization, we have Illness prevention protocols and First aid certified staff. We have an in-house nurse available to attend to any pressing medical needs.

Anti-Bullying Policy

Our measures begin with Proactive Prevention through Social-emotional Curriculum. Active Response Protocol to any bullying reports or observations and Zero tolerance after repeat incidents post numerous counselling efforts.
