About Us

Nurturing Young Minds since 2002

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe childhood should be a joyful adventure. Since 2002, we've created a nurturing space, blending purposeful play with intentional care.

Our Focus includes:

  1. Holistic Child-Growth

    2. We tend to all developmental needs.

    • We promote Cognitive Growth through play-based learning and hands-on activities.
    • Our Individualised Activities are tailored to meet the unique needs of each child.
    • We encourage social-emotional development through group activities.
  2. Bridge Children to Primary School Naturally

    3. Our reception class is more structured and mirrors primary school routine to smoothly transition to Primary school.

Safety Policies

Facility Security

Our facility has strict sign-in/sign-out procedures. Only registered parents and guardians may pick-up children.

Classroom & Playground Safety

We have Childproofed learning environments with age-appropriate equipment and Constant adult supervision.

Health & Hygiene

Besides Regular cleaning and sanitization, we have Illness prevention protocols and First aid certified staff.

Anti-Bullying Policy

Our measures begin with Proactive Prevention through Social-emotional Curriculum. Active Response Protocol to any bullying reports or observations and Zero tolerance after repeat incidents post counselling
