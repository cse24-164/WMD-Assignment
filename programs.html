Our Programs

Our curriculum is tailored to meet children's developmental needs, tending to each child's unique needs through a combination of play-based learning, age-appropriate teaching and individualised activities across the groups.

Nursery Program

Age: 18 months – 3 years

Flexible with a social-emotional basis. Offers structured learning sessions and incorporates play-based learning to improve congnitive function.

General Activities:

  • Building name awareness and Shaping Speech
  • Strengthening motor skills through scribbling, colouring
  • Building social-emotional skills: empathy, kindness, expressing feelings through group activities

Reception Class

Age: 4 – 5 years

Offers more Stucture. It's a step up from the Nursery Program and prepares children for Primary School.

Learning Objectives:

  • Learning Basic Reading and Writing
  • Have Basic Mathematical Skills
  • Have Basic Science knowledge
  • Improved cognitive, social, emotional application

Extra-Curricular Activities

Age: 3 - 5 years

Our extra-curricular activites add more fun to our children's schedule.

Activities:

  • Swimming: fun acitvity that improves motor and cognitive function
  • Computer Class: fun introduction to technology and using a Computer
