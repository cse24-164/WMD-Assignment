Admissions

Join the Pidipidi Kindergarten Community and Provide your Child with Quality education in a nurturing environment.

Admission Requirements

Admissions are open for children from 18 months to 5 years. Your child can be enrolled at any time of the year.

Registration Documents

To register, bring your child to our facilities and come prepared with:

  • Birth certificate
  • Clinic card
  • ID/Passport for the registering parent/guardian
